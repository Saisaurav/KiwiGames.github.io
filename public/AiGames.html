MENACE


Play against an AI algorithm called MENACE, and watch it gradually get better as it learns from your moves. The more games you play, the smarter it gets!

MENACE was original made in the 1960s by a British computer scientist named Donald Michie with 304 matchboxes. It stands for "Matchbox Educable Noughts And Crosses Engine".
