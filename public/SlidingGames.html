The 15 puzzle, a charming sliding puzzle all the way back from the 1880s presents a 4x4 grid with 15 numbered tiles and one empty space, where the goal is to arrange the tiles in numerical order by sliding them within the grid.

Once you know the rules, you'll grow faster and more effecient. Most people can do it in under 200 moves. Can you do it in less? Challenge your friends to beat your score!
